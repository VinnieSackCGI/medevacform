# MEDEVAC Management System
## Executive Summary for Leadership

---

### **Project Overview**

The U.S. Department of State MEDEVAC Management System is a comprehensive digital platform that revolutionizes how we handle medical evacuations for diplomatic personnel and their families worldwide. This system transforms a traditionally paper-based, labor-intensive process into a streamlined, automated workflow that enhances both efficiency and patient care outcomes.

### **Business Problem Solved**

**Current State Challenges:**
- Manual paperwork causing delays in critical medical situations
- Inconsistent data collection across global posts
- Difficulty tracking financial obligations and per diem calculations
- Limited visibility into case status and outcomes
- Compliance risks with government regulations

**Solution Delivered:**
A modern web-based system that digitizes the entire MEDEVAC process from initial request through completion, providing real-time tracking, automated calculations, and comprehensive reporting capabilities.
